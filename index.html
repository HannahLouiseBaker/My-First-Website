Foraging

When you go foraging, you might find these things:

  • Mushrooms
  • Dandelions
  • Lamb's Ear

Follow these instructions before you eat them!

  1. Make sure they're not poisonous
  2. Wash them
  3. Put them in a smoothie/stir-fry/soup
